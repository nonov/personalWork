Welcome to the University Institute Of Technology (IUT) Lyon 1 !

The IUT was created in 1967, it provides is in coherence and adapted to the work’s needs and also the economic fields. By coming to the university of Lyon 1, you will be in the 5th university of France. You also have to take into account that there are 39 000 students and 11% of them are foreigners. Lyon plays host to many foreign students every year, it’s a dynamic town where you will never get bored.

IUT

In this 2 year diploma (120 European Credits) in computer science, you will learn a lot about programming languages, web development and how a computer works.

Course objectives : The objective of the computer science courses is to form experienced professionals able to answer to clients requests .

The skilled technician operates in :

Non-computing companies, computer engineering companies (SSII), telecommunications, banks, social insurance, industries, public services, software development… They have a wide general knowledge and can also communicate with non specialist person, in English when necessary.

After the DUT you can start working or continue your studies.

CAMPUS

The IUT is a part of La Doua campus. It is located at the north of Villeurbanne, the campus is surrounded by parks: Feyssine park just next to the IUT or Tête d’Or park.

For sport, there is a sports association (SUAPS) where you can workout after school, but you can also join academic teams for high level competition.

Inside the campus you have three libraries, and a restaurant. It offers some balanced meals composed with a starter, a main course and a dessert for only 3.15€.

If you want to go to the theater with friends, l’Astrée Theater offers you a full free access for all the shows.

If you are looking for a student room, you can rent one in a building inside the campus.

For some great concerts, student parties or just events there is the double mixte hall.

LYON

The city of lights offers you its entire heritage which starts with Bellecour place, Fourvière Cathedral, the Croix-Rousse hill or the new Confluence area with some great nightclubs and restaurants.

In coming to the IUT you're also in the capital of gastronomy, some great “Bouchons Lyonnais” and restaurants await you!

It's easy to move around Lyon, with public transport. You don’t need a car, going everywhere is easy with the TCL transport network. The network connects lyon and all cities around it.

The Velo’v network is also available and cheap. A Velo’v is a rental bike, it's better to take it after a party.

Lyon is also a city of culture with a lot of museums like the new Confluence museum which will be open soon.
